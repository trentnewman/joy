她高兴地想到，他们的研究成果将会有助于帮助更多患有遗传疾病的人们。 tā gāo xìng de xiǎng dào, tā men de yán jiū chéng guǒ jiāng huì yǒu zhù yú bāng zhù gèng duō huàn yǒu yí chéng jí bìng de rén men. She thought happily that their research results will help more people suffering from genetic diseases.

李雪虽然很累，但是她感到非常满足。 lǐ xuě suī rán hěn lèi, dàn shì tā gǎn dào fēi cháng mǎn zú. Although Li Xue is tired, she feels very satisfied.

她想到自己和张博的合作，让她感到非常幸运。tā xiǎng dào zì jǐ hé zhāng bó de hé zuò, ràng tā gǎn dào fēi cháng xìng yùn. She thought about her cooperation with Zhang Bo, and felt very lucky.

李雪知道，这项研究是她人生中最重要的成就之一。 lǐ xuě zhī dào, zhè xiàng yán jiū shì tā rén shēng zhōng zuì zhòng yào de chéng jiù zhī yī. Li Xue knows that this research is one of the most important achievements of her life.
