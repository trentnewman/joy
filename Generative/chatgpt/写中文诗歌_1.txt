

(Two strangers, Yáo and Lǐ, are meeting at a train station)

Yáo: 对不起，您能告诉我这是哪一班火车吗？
duìbùqǐ, nín néng gàosu wǒ zhè shì nǎ yībān huǒchē ma?
Excuse me, can you tell me which train this is?

Lǐ: 嗯，这是D2252次列车。它去往杭州。
ēn, zhè shì D2252 cì lièchē. Tā qùwǎng Hángzhōu.
Hmm, this is the D2252 train. It goes to Hangzhou.

Yáo: 谢谢！您是哪里人？
xièxiè! Nín shì nǎlǐ rén?
Thank you! Where are you from?

Lǐ: 我是上海人。你呢？
wǒ shì Shànghǎi rén. Nǐ ne?
I'm from Shanghai. And you?

Yáo: 我是南京人。我来这里是因为我有朋友在杭州。
wǒ shì Nánjīng rén. Wǒ lái zhèlǐ shì yīnwèi wǒ yǒu péngyou zài Hángzhōu.
I'm from Nanjing. I'm here because I have a friend in Hangzhou.

Lǐ: 哦，真的吗？你们是多久以前认识的？
ó, zhēn de ma? Nǐmen shì duōjiǔ yǐqián rènshí de?
Oh, really? How long have you known each other?

Yáo: 我们是在大学认识的。已经有十年了。
wǒmen shì zài dàxué rènshí de. Yǐjīng yǒu shí nián le.
We met in university. It's been ten years.

Lǐ: 哇，真的吗？时间过得真快啊！
wā, zhēn de ma? Shíjiān guò de zhēn kuài a!
Wow, really? Time passes so quickly!
